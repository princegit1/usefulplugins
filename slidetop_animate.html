The India Today Television Group and HP Inc. India have come together to host a 'one-of-a-kind' Digital India Conclave across 9 states of the country. The regional chapters will conclude with a Grand National Digital Conclave in Delhi. Each state conclave will address the fundamental pillars of healthcare, education and essential services and governance in the host state and how Digital Technology can provide solutions to some of the key challenges in these sectors.
